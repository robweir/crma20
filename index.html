Upload your files here!

To submit, please save your files as PDFs (no JPEGs) and name the individual PDF file or files in this way: Magazine name, category number, name of category, number of files. Here is an example: (TexasMonthly35GenExcellence1of6). For spreads, please submit in spread format, not single pages.

  • There is no login for this.
  • To upload files, simply drop your files/folders into the widget itself, or click on “Browse your device” in the widget itself to browse your computer for files.
  • Progress bar shows how long you have to wait for the transfer to complete.
  • Please make sure your PDFs are saved at a minimum of 72 dpi, so the resolution will work for screens.
  • Do not worry about how large the files are.
